How to Measure Yourself

Use a soft measuring tape for accurate measurements. Measure over lightweight clothing or directly on your body.

Chest / Bust Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the ground.
Length Measure from the top of the shoulder seam down to the hem of the garment.
Shoulder Measure from shoulder point to shoulder point across the back, just below the collar.
Waist Measure around your natural waist, the narrowest part of your torso.
