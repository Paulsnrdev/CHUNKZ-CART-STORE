FAQS

Everything you need to know about shopping with Chunkz.

Orders & Payment

Browse our collections on the Shop page, add your favourite items to your cart, then click Checkout. Fill in your delivery details and complete payment via card or bank transfer through our secure Flutterwave checkout.

We accept debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer, and USSD payment via Flutterwave. All transactions are secured and encrypted.

Yes! Our store automatically detects your location and shows prices in NGN (₦), USD ($), GBP (£), or CAD (CA$). You'll always see pricing in your local currency before checkout.

Yes. Once your payment is confirmed, an order confirmation is automatically sent to our team who will follow up with you directly via the contact details you provided at checkout.

Orders are only processed after full payment is made. Once your payment clears, your order enters our production queue and is attended to in the order it came in. Please ensure payment is completed at checkout to avoid delays.

Shipping & Delivery

We currently deliver across Nigeria. International shipping to the UK, US, Canada and Other Countries are available. Please contact us via our contact page for international orders and pricing.

Domestic (Nigeria): 3–7 business days depending on your location. International orders typically take 10–21 business days. See our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

Once your order is dispatched, we'll contact you with a tracking number via WhatsApp or email. If you haven't received tracking details within 3 business days of payment, please contact us.

We attend to all orders in the exact order they come in, no order jumps the queue. This means if orders were placed before yours, they will be fulfilled first. This keeps things fair for every customer. If you need your order urgently, please see our rush order option or contact us before placing your order.

Sizing

You can select your size before placing your order by checking our Size Guide, which has full measurements for every product category from XS to XXL in both cm and inches. Simply find the size that matches your body measurements and add it to the Description / Clarity Note field at checkout.

Absolutely. If you're unsure which size to pick, you can send us your exact body measurements directly in the Description / Clarity Note field when placing your order - chest, waist, hips, height, or any relevant measurements and our team will match you to the best fit before your order is processed.

For tops, hoodies, and sweatshirts: your chest circumference and height are the most useful. For bottoms and tracksuits: your waist circumference and height. For crop tops and tanks: your bust and shoulder width. The more you include, the better we can advise you.
