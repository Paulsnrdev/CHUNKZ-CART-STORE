PRIVACY POLICY

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what data we collect and how we use it.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to Chunkz (operating at chunkzthebrand.com), an urban streetwear brand based in Nigeria. When you shop with us, we collect certain personal information to fulfil your order and improve your experience. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how we keep it safe.

By using our website or placing an order, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to process and fulfil your order.

Data Why We Collect It
Full name To address your order and package it correctly
Email address To send order confirmation and delivery updates
Phone number For delivery coordination and WhatsApp updates
Delivery address To dispatch your order to the correct location
Order details (items, size, total) To process your purchase and maintain order records
IP address / country To auto-detect your currency and delivery region
 We do not collect or store any payment card details. All payment processing is handled by Flutterwave, which has its own privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your order (packing, shipping, tracking)
  • Communicating with you about your order status via email or WhatsApp
  • Resolving any disputes, refunds, or customer service issues related to your order
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent transactions
  • Improving our website experience (using anonymised, aggregated data)

We do not use your personal data for unsolicited marketing. If we ever send promotional messages, we will only do so with your consent and will always provide a clear way to opt out.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your order data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access to order data is restricted to authorised Chunkz administrators only.

We retain your order information for a minimum of 2 years to comply with our accounting and legal obligations. After this period, data is deleted or anonymised.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no system is completely immune to security breaches. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the following parties as necessary to fulfil your order:

  • Flutterwave — Payment processing. Your payment details are sent directly to Flutterwave and never stored by us.
  • Courier / logistics partners — Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with our delivery partners solely for the purpose of delivering your order.
  • Google Firebase — Our database and hosting provider. Data is stored on Google's secure infrastructure.

We may also disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request from a government authority.

6. International Orders

If you place an international order, your delivery address and contact details will be shared with our international logistics partners. By placing an international order, you consent to your data being transferred outside of Nigeria as necessary to fulfil delivery.

We ensure that any third parties handling your data are required to maintain appropriate security measures.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations.
  • Objection — Object to us processing your data for any purpose other than order fulfilment.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our contact page. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

8. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. For full details on what cookies we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your data?

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your personal information, please don't hesitate to reach out.
