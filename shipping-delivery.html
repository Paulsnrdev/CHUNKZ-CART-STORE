Delivery FAQs

Can I change my delivery address after ordering?
Contact us as soon as possible via our contact page. If your order hasn't been dispatched yet, we can update the address.

What if no one is home when delivery is attempted?
The courier will typically leave a note or attempt redelivery the next business day. For domestic orders, you can also arrange collection from a nearby courier hub.

Do you deliver to universities and campuses?
Yes! We regularly deliver to campuses including Bowen University, ACU, Covenant University, Babcock University, PAU, Reedemers University, Veritas, Nile, OAU, FUTA, FUNNAB, OOU, UI e.t.c. Provide your full hostel/hall address and a contact number at checkout.

My order hasn't arrived — what do I do?
If your estimated delivery window has passed, contact us with your order reference and we will investigate and resolve it promptly.

Urgent / Rush Orders

Need your order faster than standard processing allows? We offer a Rush Order option for customers who need priority handling and quicker turnaround.

1
Contact Us First Reach out via WhatsApp or email before placing your order to confirm availability and your required deadline.
2
Rush Fee Applies Urgent orders attract an additional rush fee on top of the standard product and delivery price. The fee depends on your timeline and order size.
3
Place Your Order Once we've confirmed we can meet your deadline and agreed on pricing, proceed with your order as normal and note "URGENT" in the Description field.
 Rush orders are subject to availability. We process all orders in the order they are received, contacting us first ensures we can actually accommodate your timeline before you pay.
