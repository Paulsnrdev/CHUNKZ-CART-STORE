COOKIE POLICY

A plain-language explanation of the cookies we use and why.

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site.

Cookies do not contain viruses and cannot access other files on your device. They are widely used across the web to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We keep our cookie usage minimal. Here's what we use and why:

Essential Cookies Always On

These are required for the website to function. They include your shopping cart contents, selected currency, and checkout session. Without these, you cannot place an order.

Functional Cookies Optional

These remember your preferences between visits — such as your last selected currency or delivery region — so you don't have to re-enter them each time.

Analytics Cookies Optional

These help us understand how visitors use our site in aggregate (e.g. which pages are popular). Data is anonymised and never linked to your personal information.

 We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not share browsing data with ad networks or social media platforms.

3. Firebase & Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Firebase for database and authentication services. Firebase may set its own cookies or use local storage to maintain session state. These are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Our payment processor, Flutterwave, operates in a separate browser context during checkout and may set its own cookies for fraud prevention and session management. These are governed by Flutterwave's Privacy Policy.

Neither Firebase nor Flutterwave cookies are used to track you across other websites.

4. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, our website uses your browser's local storage to save your shopping cart between visits. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us until you complete a purchase. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Here's how to manage cookies in the most popular browsers:

Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent you from adding items to your cart or completing a purchase on our website.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as our website changes or to reflect new legal requirements. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page whenever changes are made. Continued use of our website after any updates means you accept the revised policy.

For details about how we handle your personal data more broadly, please see our Privacy Policy.

Questions about cookies?

If you have any questions about how we use cookies, we're happy to help.
